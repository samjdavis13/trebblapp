What is Trebbl?

Trebbl does three simple things, extremely well
Discover fresh and interesting music by browsing snippets of what your friends are listening to. Share you musical tastes with the world around you. Endorse the music you love so that it can get the recognition it deserves.

Your Direct Line To New Sounds

Discover fresh and interesting music by browsing snippets of what your friends are listening to. Once you've found something you like, simply tap through to listen to the entire song on Apple Music.

Discover the tastes of those closest to you

Endorse the music you love so that it can get the recognition it deserves. Browse this section of the app to find real time charts that have been curated by your friends and loved ones.

Share the soundtrack to your life

Share you musical tastes with the world around you. Select a portion of any song (up to 15 seconds) then post it into the world, where it can receive likes, comments and endorsements.
